A detailed background of the project

The Industrial Development in the State has put a huge strain on transport services in rural and urban areas. Similarly, two-wheelers, four-wheelers, etc. are constantly being added for faster transportation. At the same time, road congestion, increasing number of accidents, air pollution, etc. are also on the rise.

There has been a huge increase in up-to-date communication services, road vehicles, air pollution, registration of vehicles, inspections, driver's licenses, etc. The Motor Vehicles Department has been created to deal with these tasks and to implement the rules, tax laws as well as passenger tax laws etc. framed under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988.
